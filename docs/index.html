Jarvest Reminder

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Jarvest Reminder is a desktop application that helps individuals and teams track time against Jira tickets using Harvest. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

What data is accessed

Jarvest Reminder connects to two services on your behalf:

  • Jira Your assigned in-progress issues (key, summary, project, status) and your account identity to scope results to you.
  • Harvest Your time entries (read and write), project and task assignments, and your account identity.

The app only reads the minimum data required to show your active tickets and start or stop timers. It does not access your full Jira project history, other users' data, billing information, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

How data is used

Data fetched from Jira and Harvest is used solely to:

  • Display your currently in-progress Jira tickets.
  • Start, stop, and record time entries in Harvest linked to those tickets.
  • Remind you to log time during your configured work schedule.

No data is sent to any server other than Jira and Harvest. The app has no backend.

Data storage

All data — including OAuth access tokens, refresh tokens, and any cached information — is stored locally on your device only, in an encrypted store managed by the operating system. Nothing is uploaded to any external server.

Tokens are used only to authenticate API calls to Jira and Harvest. They are never logged, transmitted, or shared.

Third-party services

Jarvest Reminder uses OAuth 2.0 to connect to the following services. Each service's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data:

  • Atlassian / Jira Privacy Policy
  • Harvest Privacy Policy

Data retention and deletion

You can disconnect either service at any time from the app's Settings screen. This immediately deletes the stored tokens for that service from your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's privacy

Jarvest Reminder is a professional productivity tool not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
